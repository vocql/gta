Confirmed from Take-Two earnings – February 3, 2026

Day-One Physical + Digital Launch

Grand Theft Auto VI will launch physically and digitally on the same day, despite recent rumours suggesting otherwise. Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick confirmed: "That's not the plan" regarding any digital-only delay.

Marketing Campaign

A full marketing campaign for GTA VI is scheduled to begin this summer. Rockstar’s launch marketing beats are set to kick off, signaling strong confidence in the timeline.

Revenue Outlook

Take-Two continues to project significant revenue this year and record Net Bookings in Fiscal 2027, driven by GTA VI’s major debut in November.
