GTA VI Leaks & Details

⚠️ SPOILER WARNING ⚠️

Story

  • Chapter-based story structure like RDR2 — 5 chapters total
  • Trailer 2 footage is from Chapters 1–3
  • Jason previously served in the army
  • Lucia is the central protagonist (similar role to Michael in GTA V)
  • Lucia & Jason have separate intro missions
  • Lucia abandons her baby at a delicatessen before a bank robbery and gets arrested
  • The baby only appears in a flashback cutscene; fate unknown
  • Lucia is released on probation years later
  • Jason picks her up from prison; they later fall in love
  • Lucia removes her ankle monitor early
  • Bike → truck jump scene is from Chapter 3
  • Donnie: Jason’s friend, works for Brian, described as a “freak”

Gameplay

  • Character wheel: Solo Jason / Solo Lucia / Dual Control
  • Dual Control allows both protagonists to be used simultaneously
  • Jason has a Dead Eye–style ability (slow time + weak points)
  • Lucia can use it too, but only for one shot
  • Limited weapon system returns (weapons stored in trunk)
  • 6-star wanted level returns
  • Rectangle GPS with pink waypoints
  • Driving is reportedly the best it’s ever been
  • Greet & Antagonise system returns
  • Weight mechanics return
  • Gore & dismemberment builds on RDR2
  • Ankle monitor does not affect exploration
  • Cover system fully overhauled
  • Trailer 2 beer-buying scene is actual gameplay
  • Relationship bar returns (similar to RDR2 honor)

Missions

  • First mission: play as Jason following a plane carrying a Russian drug dealer
  • Drug heist ends with drugs falling into the ocean
  • Follow-up mission involves diving to retrieve them
  • Mission planning mainly happens in a nightclub
  • Mission where you shoot a shark with a speargun
  • Lucia infiltrates a bodybuilding gang
  • Mission involving cops betraying each other

Map

  • Map is an island again
  • No panhandle
  • Three additional islands (one inspired by the Bahamas)
  • Ambrosia & Port Gellhorn are smaller than Vice City
  • Vice City is by far the largest city
  • “Fort” city inspired by Fort Knox (gold stash)
  • Bike trail around Lake Leonida

Open World

  • 700+ shops, most interiors explorable
  • Shopping malls & skyscrapers with working elevators
  • Explorable underwater areas with secrets
  • NPCs react to visible weapons
  • NPCs call cops if you trespass
  • Basketball mini-game
  • Rentable hotels & motels
  • Male strippers included
