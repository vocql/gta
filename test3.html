DEVELOPER_LOG // MISSION_INTEL
I began coding one year ago. When news of Grand Theft Auto VI first surfaced, I became fascinated by the various countdown websites already online and decided to create my own version.

This site was built as a personal project to track the countdown to release while providing a central space for the community to follow the development and share the anticipation for the next chapter set in Leonida.
